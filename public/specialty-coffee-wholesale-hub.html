How the Sourcing Relationship Works

In The Black Coffee Co. operates as a commission-only agency. We do not mark up green coffee pricing above importer pricing — we charge a commission paid by the seller, not the buyer. This means you pay the same price for El Quetzal lots through us as you would going directly to the importer, but with the benefit of our origin relationships, lot vetting, and sample management.

What You Get

  • Direct access to El Quetzal lot availability
  • Pre-vetted lots with cup scores on file
  • Sample bags before full lot commitment
  • Transparent importer pricing
  • Harvest and arrival timing updates

Minimum Order

  • Sample bags: 150g to 500g (request via contact form)
  • Trial lots: 1 full bag (~60-70kg / 132-154 lbs)
  • Wholesale: 5+ bags preferred
  • Container: 275-300 bags (available on request)
  • No long-term contracts required

Markets We Serve

In The Black Coffee Co. works with specialty roasters across the United States. Our geographic relationships are strongest in the Midwest, but we source nationally. Roasters we work well with:

  • Specialty roasters building or expanding a Guatemala origin program
  • Roasters looking for consistent SHG supply with transparent pricing
  • Innovative roasters seeking Geisha, Java, and Anaerobic lots from Central America
  • Wholesale roasters supplying cafes who want origin-specific coffee with producer stories
  • Direct-to-consumer roasters who want to market traceable, high-altitude Guatemala lots

The El Quetzal Story

El Quetzal Coffee Co. farms at elevations above 1,600m in Guatemala's highland growing region. The farm practices shade-growing with traditional Arabica varietals alongside newer specialty varieties (Geisha, Java, Yellow Catuai). Processing is done on-site with both traditional washed and experimental methods (honey, anaerobic). The operation is small-scale and relationship-oriented, which is why sample-first buying is standard practice — we want you to be confident in what you're buying before you commit to a lot.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is In The Black Coffee Co.?
In The Black Coffee Co. is a commission-only wholesale coffee sourcing agency specializing in specialty-grade green coffee from Guatemala. We work directly with El Quetzal Coffee Co. and broker their lots to specialty roasters across North America.
Is there a minimum order for green coffee?
Sample bags are available in 150g-500g quantities for roasters evaluating a new lot. Trial orders start at one full bag (~60-70kg). Preferred wholesale volumes are 5+ bags. Container-level purchasing (275-300 bags) is available on request.
How does commission-only sourcing work?
In The Black Coffee Co. charges a commission paid by the seller (El Quetzal / importer), not the buyer. Roasters pay importer pricing without an agency markup. Our value is origin relationships, pre-vetted lots, sample management, and transparent pricing — not margin on top of green coffee cost.
What Guatemala coffee lots are available?
Current lots include SHG Washed, Yellow Catuai, Java, and limited availability Geisha and Anaerobic Java from El Quetzal Coffee Co.'s Guatemalan highlands. Contact In The Black for current inventory and pricing.
